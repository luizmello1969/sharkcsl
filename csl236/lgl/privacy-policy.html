Overview

Welcome to Prozenith. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle and protect your personal information when you visit our website [www.getprozenith.com/cpda] ("the Site"). Acceptance and Updates

By accessing or using our Site, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy. Prozenith reserves the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy. Any updates will be posted with a revised "Effective Date," and continued use of our Site signifies your acceptance of these changes.

Information We Collect

We may collect both personally identifiable information (PII) and non-personally identifiable information (non-PII):

  • Personal Information: Information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, postal address, payment details, and any other data you submit when registering, making purchases, or subscribing to our services.
  • Automatic Data Collection: Data such as your device's IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and browsing activity may be collected to improve your experience and monitor traffic on our Site. This data may be gathered through cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect allows us to:

  • Process orders and manage your account.
  • Improve the functionality and content of our Site.
  • Provide personalized experiences and offers tailored to your interests.
  • Communicate with you regarding promotions, updates, and services.
  • Share relevant advertisements through various channels, such as email, SMS, or direct mail.

You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by following the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or contacting us directly.

Cookies and Web Beacons

We utilize cookies (small data files stored on your device) and web beacons (clear images embedded on our website or emails) to track your interactions, enhance your user experience, and gather information about browsing behavior. You have the ability to manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Our Site may include links to external websites or advertisements managed by third-party advertisers. These third parties may collect data through cookies, pixels, or other tracking technologies. Prozenith is not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites, and we recommend reviewing their privacy policies if you have any concerns.

Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal data with trusted third-party partners, such as service providers, to facilitate services on our behalf (e.g., payment processing, shipping, marketing assistance). These partners are obligated to handle your data securely and use it only for the purposes specified by Prozenith.

In the event of any cooperative services or promotions, information may also be shared with relevant partners.

Legal Obligations and Business Transfers

We may disclose your information as required by law, legal proceedings, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Prozenith and our users. In the case of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

Google Analytics and Facebook Integration

Our Site uses Google Analytics to help us analyze visitor behavior and optimize our marketing strategies. The features we use may include:

GDPR and CCPA Compliance

While Prozenith primarily serves U.S. customers, we respect the rights of users under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • GDPR: If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@getprozenith.com.
  • CCPA: California residents can request access to or deletion of their personal information, or opt out of data sharing. Contact us at support@getprozenith.com to make such requests.

Your personal information is stored on secure servers, and we take reasonable precautions to protect it from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
